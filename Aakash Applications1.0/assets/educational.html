Educational Content

Apart from displaying text files in various formats, Aakash can play video and audio content. The content can be either on a local Micro SD card storage or on a server accessible through Wi-Fi. A lot of contents are available through efforts such as NPTEL, Spoken Tutorials, Workshop courses, etc. Wikipedia, Gutenberg project, Connexions project at Rice University, and many such global efforts have generated a large pool of knowledge contents in Open Source.

The efforts at IIT Bombay have been to add more such contents for use in Indian education system, to integrate these together in a better usable format, and to make these accessible using Aakash.

This application accesses such contents, located on an SD card, in .pdf, .html, or .mp3 formats.
